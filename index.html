About Bacon Pancakes

Have you ever watched a series, movie, or played a game and salivated over an absolutely mouthwatering-looking meal? Us too- and being the problem-solving, overthinking nerds we have created a digital platform in which anyone can look up and cook up fictional meals! We believe all recipes from media should be able to be cooked in real life and enjoyed by it's fans. Why are we called Bacon Pancakes? We beg of you to go watch Adventure Time if you do not get the reference. And promptly return to make the iconic meal with our guide.
